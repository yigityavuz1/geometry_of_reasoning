\section{Abstract}
This report studies training-free geometric and uncertainty signals for early detection of reasoning derailment in LLM chain-of-thought trajectories.

\section{Method}
Core signals include local intrinsic dimension (MLE, TwoNN, ABID), participation ratio, and conditional entropy, measured at step boundaries.

\section{Experiments}
We evaluate early warning, model comparison, and estimator sensitivity under a controlled protocol on GSM8K.
